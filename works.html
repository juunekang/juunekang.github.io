A Python program that when given a DNA sequence will generate the sequence of the complementary strand and find the longest "gene" for that DNA, transcribes the gene into mRNA, and tranlates the mRNA into an amnio acid sequence. Thorough research was conducted and the link will open up a folder that includes a research paper and the program itself.
